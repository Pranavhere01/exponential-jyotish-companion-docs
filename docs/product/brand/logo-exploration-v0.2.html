Rebuilt from product truth · v0.2

A trusted jyoti, not another horoscope badge.

Jyotish Companion is a private AI astrologer that keeps chart math deterministic and interpretation humane. The brand should feel like a careful Vedic advisor with a modern instrument: precise, calm, old-world, and personal enough to be a daily companion.

What changed

This version removes generic zodiac glyphs, glowing app-store mandalas, random stars, and ornamental AI polish. The new system uses more specific cues: jyoti, North Indian kundali structure, palm-leaf manuscripts, dasha timing, memory beads, temple thresholds, and brass-instrument geometry.

Product promise

Chart facts are computed, not guessed. The logo should therefore feel measured and architected, not floaty.

User feeling

A seeker should feel privately guided, not sold to. Warmth matters as much as mystique.

Premium cue

Think brass, ink, sandalwood paper, and observatory tools instead of neon spirituality.

Ownable idea

The strongest territory is the jyoti inside the kundali: insight held inside structure.

Reject list

  • No zodiac-symbol soup.
  • No generic lotus-plus-circuit clip art.
  • No excessive glow pretending to be premium.
  • No “fortune teller” tone.
  • No fear-selling symbols or doom cues.

New design brief

  • Anchor every mark in one product truth: computed chart, humane guide, consent memory, timing, or reviewed knowledge.
  • Design each concept as a system: app mark, wordmark, report seal, and tiny UI glyph should all be possible.
  • Favor slightly imperfect, editorial, brass-and-ink character over shiny synthetic geometry.
  • Use Vedic signals with restraint: kundali, jyoti, sutra, temple threshold, dasha cycle, manuscript.
