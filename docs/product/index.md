# Product

The product domain owns user-facing intent: strategy, requirements, scope, PM decisions, and non-technical explainers.

## Canonical Artifacts

- [Product Way Forward](strategy/way-forward.md)
- [PRD v0.2](requirements/prd-v0.2.md)
- [PM First-Principles Guide](guides/pm-first-principles-guide.md)
- [Brand And Logo Exploration](brand/index.md)

## Ownership

Primary owner: Product  
Required reviewers when changed: Engineering for feasibility, AI Quality for model behavior, Governance for durable decisions.

## Belongs Here

- Product vision and positioning.
- Brand identity, naming direction, logo exploration, and visual positioning.
- User segments, jobs, and workflows.
- In-scope and out-of-scope decisions.
- Roadmap direction and PM-readable technical explanations.

## Does Not Belong Here

- Raw schema DDL.
- Prompt source files.
- Deployment runbooks.
- ADRs, unless linked as decision context.
