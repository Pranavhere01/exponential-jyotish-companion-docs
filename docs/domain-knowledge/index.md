# Domain Knowledge

The domain knowledge area owns reviewed subject-matter material that can eventually feed product retrieval.

This is separate from product requirements and architecture. Product docs say what we are building; domain knowledge stores reviewed doctrine, source maps, chunking policy, topic taxonomy, and retrieval policy for the knowledge system.

## Current Areas

- [Jyotish](jyotish/index.md)

## Ownership

Primary owner: Product + AI Quality

Required reviewers when changed: Domain reviewer, Product, AI Quality, Governance for policy changes.

## Belongs Here

- Jyotish source maps and provenance notes.
- Reviewed doctrine summaries.
- Topic and taxonomy design for knowledge chunks.
- Delivery-policy guidance for domain material.
- Curation workflow notes that affect retrievable knowledge.

## Does Not Belong Here

- User memories or user chart facts.
- Raw unreviewed source dumps.
- Product roadmap prose.
- Database migrations.
- Prompt source files.
