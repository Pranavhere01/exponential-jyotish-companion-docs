# Governance

The governance domain owns durable decisions, standards, and source-of-truth policy.

## Current Areas

- [Decision Records](decisions/index.md)
- [Standards](standards/index.md)

## Ownership

Primary owner: Product + Engineering leads  
Required reviewers when changed: Anyone accountable for the decision being recorded.

## Belongs Here

- ADRs.
- Standards and policies.
- Source-of-truth rules.
- Durable choices that future contributors must understand.

## Does Not Belong Here

- Day-to-day runbooks.
- Raw source materials.
- Draft brainstorms without durable decision value.
